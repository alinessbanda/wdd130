White Water Rafting Site Plan

Site Name

White Water Rafting Company

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote a rafting adventure company that provides safe, exciting white water rafting experiences. Users can explore trips, learn about the company, and contact the business for bookings.

Target Audience

  • Adventure seekers
  • Tourists
  • Families and groups
  • Corporate team-building groups

Site Map

  • Home (index.html)
  • About Us (about.html)
  • Trips (trips.html)
  • Contact Us (contact.html)

Page Descriptions

Home Page

Introduces the company with hero images, call-to-action, and featured experiences.

About Us

Explains company history, mission, and team information.

Trips

Shows rafting packages, difficulty levels, and adventure details.

Contact Us

Includes a contact form and company contact information.

Branding

Theme: Adventure / Outdoor / Nature

Font: Roboto + Georgia

#1a73e8 Primary Blue
#fbbc05 Accent Yellow

#34a853 Green

Navigation

Each page will include navigation linking to all pages and a return link to the main portfolio page.
